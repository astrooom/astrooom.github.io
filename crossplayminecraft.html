How does it work?

Minecraft Crossplay uses GeyserMC to bridge the gap between Minecraft: Bedrock Edition and Minecraft: Java Edition servers.

GeyserMC translates packets from Bedrock Edition clients to work seamlessly with Java Edition Servers - without you having to configure anything. It just works!

You can use any Spigot/Paper/Bukkit plugin for your server, installable just like on a Paper or Spigot Minecraft: Java Edition server.

Questions & Answers

Can I use my already existing Minecraft world?

Yes you can! To do so, please follow this guide after your purchase.

How does this work?

It's quite simple! After you have made your purchase, you will recieve an email with a username and a password to our server panel (username and password can be changed after first-login). On the panel, you can start, stop and restart your server, as well as modify files and settings, should you wish to. You are welcome to use a free trial to see how it all works.

Can I cancel my server at any time?

Yes you can. There are never any contracts, obligations or hidden fees with our services. We take customer satisfaction extremely seriously.

How long is the setup time for your servers?

It's very fast! Your Minecraft instance will be provisioned and ready to start within 5 minutes after your purchase thanks to our automated setup mechanism.

Can I use plugins on my server?

Yes! Spigot/Paper/Bukkit plugins can be installed in the plugins folder on your server and are fully usable on both Java and Bedrock platforms.
