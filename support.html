Frequently Asked Questions

When will my server be ready?

Our Servers are instantly created upon purchase. If you are having issues, create a ticket on the panel and we will get things running smoothly as fast as possible.

How do I get support for my server?

Simply submit a ticket on the panel and we will get back to you as soon as possible.

How do I connect to the server?

To connect to your server, login to the panel and click on your newly created server. From there, you can see the connection adress and manage the server should you want to. Paste the adress in to your game client (minecraft, terraria, etc...) and connect. Upon purchase, your server details will also be emailed to the adress you've registered with on the panel.

Can I transfer my existing server files/worlds/maps or a single player world to Labradorhost?

Yes! We provide full server file access through the panel and with SFTP. If you don't know how to transfer the world files, please submit a ticket on the panel and we will do it for you.

How do I change server version or modpack?

To change server version or modpack, go to the "Versions" tab on your server console page. Here, simply select which version or modpack you want to install. NOTE: Switching to certain versions will require you to select the "Delete Files" option. If you wish to keep your server files, you can make a backup in the "backup" tab and restore.

Do I need to use portforwarding for my server?

No. We make it very easy to get started. You won't need any advanced technical knowledge to get started playing. Simply enter the adress you recieved after purchase to you game client and connect.

What types of server versions can I use?

We provide a wide range of different server versions for our services. For Minecraft alone we provide one-click installation of the latest verison 61 different modpacks (and counting!). For Terraria we support both TShock and tModLoader.

Can I use plugins?

Absolutely! You have full access to all your server files on the panel and via SFTP.

Can I use MYSQL database with your service?

Yes! Simply go to the "Databases" section of your server panel and create one.

How do I make a backup of my server?

To make a backup of your server, go to the "Backup" tab your server panel and create a backup. You can download your backup to your own system should you want to.

What uptime do you provide?

Our servers have an uptime of over 99.9%. The most common cause of a crashed server is a faulty plugin or mod which we can help resolve.

Are my worlds and files saved if my server expires?

Yes. Your server files will be saved for a period of 3 months if your server expires. The server will be in a "suspended" state, but will not be deleted. You can renew at any time and resume where you left off.

Do you offer sponsorships?

Sure! If you have a following on plattforms such as Youtube or Twitch, contact us via the question form below or at support@labradorhost.com and we will review your request.

Do you provide extra ports for your servers?

Yes. If your server requires the use of extra ports, they should be visible on the panel already. If they aren't, hit us up and we will provide them for you.

How do I change the player limit on my Minecraft Server?

Enter your server control panel and navigate to the "File Manager" section. Here, press on your server.properties file and edit the "max-players" option to the limit you want.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 72 hour money back guarantee. If you're not happy with your purchase or simply changed your mind, just submit a ticket on the panel - all we ask if that you let us know why so we can improve our service for the future.

How much disk space is available on my server?

Unlimited. You can use as much space as you want as long as the files are related to your service.

How do I change settings (difficulty, world size, version) on my Terraria dedicated server?

The easiest way to do this is to go to the "Startup" tab in your server panel and restart the server once your changes have been made. You can also access the server files via the File Manager on the panel and SFTP and manage settings there.

My Xonotic server won't start because I don't have a server.cfg. How do I fix this?

Xonotic servers require you to create your own config the first time you start them. To do this, please check: https://github.com/xonotic/xonotic/blob/master/server/server.cfg for detailed instructions.

Are your servers on 24/7?

Yes! Our servers don't sleep.

Can I cancel my server at any time?

Yes. There are no contracts or obligations if you wish to cancel.

Can I change my server hosting plan or package?

Yes. Please submit a ticket on the panel and let us know which package you would like to change to. We will be able to complete the change within a few hours without changing your servers address.
