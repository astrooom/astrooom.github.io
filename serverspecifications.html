Our servers use Intel® Core™ i9-9900K Octa-Core processors or equivalent to make sure our clients have no lag-related issues running their game and voice servers.

The Intel® Core™ i9-9900K has a base frequency of 3.6 GHz and can boost up to a whopping 5.0 GHz if needed. This means that our servers can handle resource intensive tasks such as a server with a lot of players on at the same time.


All of our servers run on professional-grade DDR4 RAM to make sure the RAM can keep up with your server to the fullest extend.

All of our servers use proffesional-grade NVMe SSD Storage for blazing-fast drive performance. The drives run in a software RAID 1 Configuration.

All of our servers run on blazing-fast 1Gbps Fiber Connections with DDoS Protection.
None of our services have any bandwith-limitations.

All of our servers use Debian 10 (Stretch) to make sure there is as little overhead as possible when running servers on our hardware.
