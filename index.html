What is Labradorhost?

Labradorhost is a fairly new Game and Voice hosting service run by gaming enthusiasts who have been actively playing games throughout the 2000s.

The creators of this service are freelancers. This allows for scalability in what we can offer to our customers, which we think is vital to running a hosting service with many users. We always look forward to hearing from our customers and to fullfill potential requests.

You may be asking to yourself – why should I go with such a new hosting service? Well, our prices are hard to beat, and our support is always friendly. We suggest giving us a chance and can assure you that you won't be disappointed. In addition, the 72-hour refund policy applies to all services in case you, for any reason, aren't satisfied. We take customer satisfaction extremely seriously.
